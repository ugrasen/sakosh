Health Care

Our Professional Aging Life Care Managers specialize in providing comprehensive aging life care management services, patient advocacy, and assisting elders and their families with planning for and meeting long-term care needs. Care Managers at The Cameron Group have extensive experience and training in gerontology, social work, nursing, and counseling and provide services such as:

  • Assessing clients' physical, psychological, social, and emotional needs.
  • Appointment reminders, Coordinating and managing care and services to help older adults thrive at home or in facilities.
  • Arranging, screening, and monitoring in/out-home help, such as companions or home health aides.
  • Overseeing the quality of care provided in the home or in a facility.
  • Coordinating any transitions or residence moves.
  • Reviewing financial, legal, and medical issues and offering safe referrals to qualified professionals.
  • Offering counseling and emotional support.
  • Advocating for consistent adherence to the clients' wishes and values within complex medical systems and throughout the aging process.

24 X 7 Companionship

Every client has an advocate on call 24/7 committed to client dignity and comfort. Your Care Coordinator knows your loved one's care plan, needs, schedule, likes, and dislikes. He will ensure that care givers are available around the clock to manage emergencies. Single point of contact for any requirement.

Additionally, each Caring Companion uses our cutting-edge technology to keep families informed about their loved one's status. Using daily reports, real-time updates, and photos, a Caring Companion gives you the information you need — a "window into the living room" so you can relax and know your loved one is safe and comfortable.

Wouldn't it be great if you could count on someone else to find the right companions with the right skills for your family? And worry about finding a backup when your regular companion is sick or on vacation?

Our client care coordinators select companions to match your family's needs. We provide the right companion, at the right time - with no unpleasant surprises. We are on call 24 x 7 for emergencies so you can sleep through the night.

Food & Catering Services

Once a senior parent or disabled family member can no longer cook a nourishing meal for themselves, it won't be long before their health suffers. As part of our home care meal service, our care givers can prepare complete, nutritionally balanced breakfasts, lunches and dinners with the right portion sizes for good health.

As per requirement our coordinator will provide special diet to the parent.
On special occasion we will be treat them with special diet considering all the health issues and doctor's recommendation.
Our coordinator will be keeping separate prescription records and as per that they will be serving the food to the parents.

Social activities

Recent studies have proven that boredom and depression can have a very real effect on physical, emotional and mental health. So to avoid boredom, our team will look for some scope to entertain the parents.

We will try to gift them some wonderful moments with some entertainments like celebrating their Birthday, Anniversary, or taking them to religious places, or involving them in some social.

We would also like to arrange some party or get together at house and bring the close people so that parents can have a wonderful day.
